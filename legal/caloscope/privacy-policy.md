Caloscope ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Data Collection and Storage

### 1.1 Local Storage (Privacy First)

Caloscope is designed with a "privacy-first" approach. **All your caloric data, meal logs, weight history, and personal settings are stored locally on your device.** We do not host servers that store your personal health data.

### 1.2 No Account Registration

You do not need to create an account or provide an email address to use Caloscope. Your data is tied to your physical device.

### 1.3 Data Portability

You have full control over your data. You can export your data to a JSON file at any time using the Backup feature. You are responsible for the security of your exported files if you choose to store them in cloud services (like Google Drive) or external storage.
