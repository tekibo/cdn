# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 1, 2026**

At GrowPad, developed by TEKIBO, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or set up your profile, we may collect:

- **Name**: To personalize your experience.
- **Email Address**: For authentication and account recovery.
- **Phone Number (Optional)**: For contact purposes.
- **Job Position**: To tailor the app's features to your role.
- **"Heard About Us" Source**: To understand our growth.

### 1.2 Authentication Data

We use Firebase Authentication (Google and Email/Password) to manage your account. This may involve collecting your unique user ID and basic profile information from Google if you choose to sign in with your Google account.

### 1.3 Application Data

The data you enter into GrowPad (workspaces, roadmaps, tasks, financial data, marketing plans, etc.) is primarily stored **locally on your device**.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the GrowPad service.
- Authenticate your access to the app.
- Synchronize your user profile across devices via Firestore.
- Improve our application based on user feedback and usage patterns.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Your startup-specific data (tasks, finances, etc.) is stored in a secure local database on your device.
- **Cloud Storage**: Your user profile information is securely stored and synchronized using Firebase (Google Cloud).
- **Security**: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 4. Third-Party Services

GrowPad uses Firebase, a service provided by Google, for:

- Authentication (Firebase Auth)
- Database Synchronization (Cloud Firestore)

Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more details on how they handle data.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request the correction of inaccurate data.
- Request the deletion of your account and associated profile data.
