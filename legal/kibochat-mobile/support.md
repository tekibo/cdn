## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Is my data safe?
Yes, KiboChat is a privacy-first application. All your data, including chat history and documents, stays locally on your device.

### Do I need an internet connection?
An internet connection is only required to download models from the Model Library. Once downloaded, the AI chat and RAG features work entirely offline.

### Which models are supported?
KiboChat supports GGUF models, which can be easily managed within the app's Model Library.
